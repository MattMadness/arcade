Flying Dog by Matthew Evan

Enter the world of Flying Dog! The most annoying game you will EVER play.
When a dog learns how to fly, fans swarm him, with experimental syringes for experiments, for Doctor Zapman! Use arrow keys to move, down to stomp, and space to fire bones! Bones may come out you mouth or, the other end. Enjoy!
